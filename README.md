## 📊 Sales Report

- **Project Objectives:**  
    **1.** Build a detailed _[Customer Performance Report](https://github.com/harshalfrancis/data-analysis-case-study/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_ to analyze customer‑level trends and growth.  
    **2.** Develop a _[Market Performance vs Target Report](https://github.com/harshalfrancis/data-analysis-case-study/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target%20Report.pdf)_ to compare actual performance against sales goals.

- **Purpose of Sales Analytics:**  
    Provide clear visibility into sales performance, helping businesses understand what drives revenue and where improvements can be made.

- **Why Sales Data Matters:**  
    - Reveals buying patterns  
    - Highlights high‑value customers  
    - Tracks KPIs such as revenue, margin, and growth  

- **How These Reports Help:**  
    - Support discount and pricing decisions  
    - Strengthen negotiation strategies  
    - Identify markets with strong expansion potential

## 💰 Finance Report

- **Project Objectives:**  
    **1.** Create Profit & Loss (P&L) statements by _[P&L Statement by Fiscal Year](https://github.com/harshalfrancis/data-analysis-case-study/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)_ and _[P&L Statement by Month](https://github.com/harshalfrancis/data-analysis-case-study/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf)_ to understand financial trends.  
    **2.** Build P&L reports by _[P&L Statement by Market](https://github.com/harshalfrancis/data-analysis-case-study/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)_ to evaluate regional profitability.

- **Purpose of Financial Analytics:**  
    Offer a clear view of financial health, enabling better planning, forecasting, and communication with stakeholders.

- **Why Finance Data Matters:**  
    - Supports budgeting and forecasting  
    - Helps benchmark performance across periods  
    - Identifies cost patterns and profitability drivers  

- **How These Reports Help:**  
    - Align financial decisions with strategic goals  
    - Improve transparency and confidence in financial planning
